"The Base" was a dedicated sub-event during the 2026 Saint George Days festival (May 1-3), designed to create a unique cultural and social hub within the larger celebration. My role was comprehensive, spanning from initial marketing strategy and graphic identity to on-site team management and content creation. I led the social media presence across all platforms, managed the photography and videography team, and personally captured key moments to produce the final aftermovie. This project was a complete end-to-end exercise in event branding and live digital execution.
